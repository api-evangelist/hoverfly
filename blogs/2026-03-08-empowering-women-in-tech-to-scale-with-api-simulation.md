This International Women’s Day marks the 115th time the world has come together to celebrate. To put that into perspective, when the first IWD was observed in 1911, the computer was still a human job title, and the concept of a cloud was strictly meteorological. Fast forward to 2026, and the software development landscape has changed beyond our wildest imagination. At Hoverfly Cloud, we spend our days helping teams simulate real APIs to accelerate development testing. But there is one thing we never simulate: our respect for the women technology leaders setting new frontiers.
