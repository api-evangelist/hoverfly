Testing scalability for high-volume environments The bottleneck for scaling isn’t always your code; it’s the dependencies you rely on. For enterprise organisations, the challenge of high-volume traffic is a year-round reality. To maintain resilience, teams must move beyond simple mocking toward a robust environment where they can simulate their existing APIs under true production-level stress. Why basic mocking fails at scale However, as organisations scale, these unmanaged tools often reveal a performance gap . Basic mocks rarely account for the infrastructure friction or the latency issues that occur when a system is needs to handle thousands of concurrent requests. To de-risk your environment, you need an enterprise-grade approach to application testing that can handle: High throughput: Supporting up to 2,000 requests per second per instance to mirror real-world surges Load-balanced clusters: Ensuring your simulation environment doesn’t become the bottleneck during peak stress testing Realistic dependency simulation: Virtualising third-party dependencies so your internal teams aren’t left waiting for external API access Managed infrastructure: Utilising a platform managed by a specialist partner like iOCO Cloud who are also experts in cloud migration and modernisation to provide the technical reassurance CTOs and Architects require.
