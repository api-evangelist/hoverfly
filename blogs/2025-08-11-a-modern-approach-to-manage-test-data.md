Interconnected systems are the norm and they’re becoming more complex than ever. Whether it’s a website, a mobile app, and a CRM all needing to be in sync, ensuring that test data is aligned and consistent across all these systems can be a time-consuming and costly exercise. For Quality Assurance Managers , the dependency on unstable or unavailable APIs can lead to slow and unpredictable test cycles.
